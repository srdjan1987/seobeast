Wildfire Range Pizza Ovens
Introducing the Alfresco Wildfire range, suitable for modern alfresco areas
Due to the increasing demand for a traditional wood-fired oven that complements more modern areas, we have designed the Alfresco Wildfire range. This range of ovens features a polished granite entrance arch and coasted black steel or stainless steel door option, which complements the stainless steel appliances that often feature in modern alfresco areas. The Alfresco Wildfire Range comes in three different sizes – the Courtyard, the Midi and the Original – and five different render colours.
